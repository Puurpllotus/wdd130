Activity Description
Rafting Rafting is an adventurous outdoor activity that involves navigating through rivers or other bodies of water using an inflatable raft. It is often done on whitewater rivers, where the flow of water creates rapids and obstacles that add excitement and challenge to the experience.
Kayaking Kayaking is a water sport that involves paddling a small, narrow watercraft known as a kayak.
Rock Climbing Rock climbing is an outdoor activity that involves climbing up, down, or across natural rock formations or artificial rock walls.
